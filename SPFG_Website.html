About

Built by Someone Who Has Worked the Department

Sterile Processing Field Guide exists because too many SPD departments — especially small and rural ones — are trying to build education programs with almost no support.

Why This Exists

Real SPD Education for Real Departments

Too many new SPD technicians are handed a binder and told to figure it out. Too many rural facilities are operating without structured training programs. Too many new leads are promoted without any kind of leadership support.

Sterile Processing Field Guide was created to fill that gap — with practical, plainspoken resources that are actually useful in a real department on a real day.

Every resource is built around how SPD actually works: the workflow, the documentation, the regulatory requirements, and the real-world constraints that smaller facilities face. Nothing is theoretical. Nothing is designed for a level-one academic medical center with a full education department.

This is for the two-person SPD team at a rural surgery center. It's for the OR nurse who just got cross-trained. It's for the new lead who has never written an SOP before. It's for the technician who wants to understand the why behind what they do every day.

Our Commitment

What We Stand Behind

  • Content aligned with AAMI, SGNA, AORN, and CDC guidance
  • Manufacturer IFU compliance emphasized throughout all materials
  • No overclaiming — we are educational, not certifying
  • Practical first — if it doesn't work in a real department, we fix it
  • Accessible language without talking down to professionals
  • Resources that work for small teams with limited budgets

What We Are Not

We are not an accrediting body, a certification program, or a regulatory compliance service. Our materials support your facility's education program — they do not replace your policies, your IFUs, your accreditation obligations, or your employer's competency validation requirements.
